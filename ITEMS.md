# Complete Item List

A comprehensive guide to all items included in Eldertide Armaments.

## Rings (13 Total)

### Ring 1 - Dragonsoul Ring
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Value**: 750 gold
- **Abilities Unlocked**:
  - Dragonkin Heritage (Zone)
  - Skyward Soar (Projectile/Flight)
- **Passive**: Draconic Retaliation
- **Build Focus**: Dragon-themed characters, flight mobility
- **Power Tier**: High

## Item Statistics

### Rarity Distribution

- **Legendary Items**: 10 total (7 rings, 3 amulets)
- **Very Rare Items**: 12 total (6 rings, 6 amulets)
- **Total Equippable Items**: 22 (13 rings + 9 amulets)

### Value Ranges

**Rings**:
- Highest: 950g (Ring 11 - Death's Dominion)
- Lowest: 500g (Ring 5, Ring 10)
- Average: ~725g

**Amulets**:
- Highest: 950g (Amulet 8 - Witcher's Medallion)
- Lowest: 400g (Amulet 4 - Sage's Amulet)
- Average: ~722g

### Ability Score Bonuses

**Strength**: Rings 7, 9, 13; Amulet 1
**Dexterity**: Rings 3, 5; Amulet 3
**Constitution**: Ring 10; Amulet 1
**Intelligence**: Amulet 4
**Wisdom**: Rings 12; Amulet 6
**Charisma**: Rings 2; Amulet 9

### Special Features

**Critical Hit Auto-Success**: Rings 11; Amulet 1
**Dual Passives**: Rings 9, 12; Amulet 8
**Most Abilities**: Ring 13 (4 abilities), Ring 2 (4 abilities)
**Ritual Magic**: Amulet 4 (5 ritual spells)
**Complex System**: Amulet 8 (Witcher Signs system)

## Finding Items

### Tutorial Chest Version
- All items available in tutorial chest
- Requires Tutorial Chest Summoning mod
- Instant access to all equipment

### Immersive Version
Items distributed across ~40 locations:

**High-End Merchants**:
- Sorcerous Sundries (Lower City)
- Harper Quartermaster (Last Light)
- Githyanki Quartermaster

**Special Chests**:
- Ketheric's Personal Belongings
- Hidden Room Magic Chests
- Boss Treasure Rooms

**Quest Rewards**:
- Major boss encounters
- Hidden area discoveries

**Random Loot**:
- Opulent gold stashes
- Wealthy treasure caches

See INSTALLATION.md for detailed location information.
